A Traditionally Animated Fighting Game

Experience fluid combat, dynamic characters, and breathtaking environments in this unique fighting game.

GAME CONCEPT

Blending Traditional Fighting with Platform Brawler Mechanics

Unique Hybrid Combat

Rising Spirit is a 2D traditional fighting game with the feel of a platform brawler smoothly blended together to create its own experience and game type. This fusion grants players the freedom and flexibility within the playstyle of one character instead of feeling confined to the "Developer's Intent."

  • Accessible yet deep combat system
  • Freedom to discover unique strategies
  • Fluid movement and responsive controls
  • Dynamic environment interaction

Player Engagement

Rising Spirit is designed in such a way that you will be learning, figuring out and discovering new strategies and ways to play the more you play the game. It is a game where both Brawler and Traditional fighter communities can find common ground.

Our Philosophy

"Easy to pick up but hard to master. Create a fighting game where players have room to express their unique style through each character."

CHARACTERS

Meet the Cast of Rising Spirit

Character System

Rising Spirit features a unique cast (currently showing 4, planning for 10), each with their own fighting styles, elemental affinities, and personal stories. Each character has been designed to offer distinctive gameplay opportunities while maintaining competitive balance.

Character Development

Each character in Rising Spirit is the result of careful design consideration, with unique move sets that reflect their personality and background. Characters represent various elemental powers: Fire, Water, Air, and Earth.

GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

Smooth Combat System with Unique Elements

Combat System

Our smooth 60 FPS combat system blends the tight control of traditional fighters with the freedom of platform brawlers, creating a unique fighting experience.

  • Responsive controls
  • Combo flexibility
  • Aerial maneuvers
  • Ground techniques

Shield Mechanics

The unique shield system in Rising Spirit adds strategic depth, allowing players to defend against attacks in various ways.

  • Moveable shields
  • Full shield defense
  • Parry opportunities
  • Shield meter management

Movement System

Freedom of movement is a core aspect of Rising Spirit, with a context-sensitive running mechanic and versatile movement options.

  • Dashing and running
  • Wall jumps
  • Air recovery
  • Head tracking for animations

Advanced Mechanics

Combo System

Rising Spirit features a dynamic combo system that allows players to discover new combinations and strategies as they play. The system provides:

  • Cancel points for extended combos
  • Multi-hit attacks with strategic applications
  • Air-to-ground and ground-to-air combo potential
  • Character-specific combo paths

Elemental System

Each character harnesses the power of one of four elements: Fire, Water, Air, and Earth. These elements influence:

  • Special move properties and visual effects
  • Strategic matchup considerations
  • Environmental interactions on certain stages
  • Story and character development
